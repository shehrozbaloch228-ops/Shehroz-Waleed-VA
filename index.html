Execution Protocols

Standard Operating Procedures (SOP & SLA Accordion).

Every daily virtual assistant function is governed by strict, unyielding operational checklists. Click any protocol below to inspect the step-by-step execution methodology.

Execution Frequency: Every Morning at 08:00 AM CST precisely.

1. Open Brand Registry Infringement portal and scan all active US catalog ASINs for unauthorized piggybackers or malicious offer duplications.

2. Cross-reference unauthorized seller IDs against verified distributor MAP agreements.

3. Dispatch automated Cease & Desist legal warnings via Amazon Buyer-Seller messaging; escalate persistent violators to Brand Registry test-buys within 4 hours.

Execution Frequency: Every 1st and 15th of the month.

1. Extract closed FBA shipment logs from Seller Central Fulfillment reports and compare 'Shipped' vs. 'Received' unit quantities.

2. Audit carrier proof of delivery (POD), stamped bills of lading (BOL), and commercial commercial invoices.

3. Open formal Seller Central reimbursement cases for all unit discrepancies; track Case IDs until cash or inventory credit posts to the ledger.

Execution Frequency: Continuous Real-Time SLA (< 2 hour response).

1. Monitor Account Health Rating (AHR) dashboard for Order Defect Rate (ODR) spikes or immediate buyer claim submittals.

2. Compile carrier tracking logs, delivery GPS coordinates, and buyer communication history into bulleted defense templates.

3. Submit professional claim representations to Amazon policy teams to successfully defend seller funds and maintain a pristine 100% AHR score.

Execution Frequency: Weekly on Monday Afternoons.

1. Download Stranded Inventory reports and Fix Your Products (FYP) suppression logs.

2. Re-link disconnected SKU attributes, correct missing bullet points, and re-upload compliant 1000x1000px main white-background images.

3. Validate that 100% of brand ASINs are actively indexable across U.S. customer postal codes.
